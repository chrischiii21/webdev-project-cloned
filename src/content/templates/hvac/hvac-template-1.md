### Implementation Instructions

1. **Seasonal Messaging**: Update seasonal maintenance schedules based on local climate
2. **Service Packages**: Customize maintenance packages and pricing tiers
3. **Technician Photos**: Replace stock photos with actual team member photos
4. **Service Area Map**: Update coverage area and response times
5. **Warranty Information**: Adjust warranty terms based on client policies

### Key Features

- **Seasonal Campaigns**: Spring/Fall maintenance reminders
- **Package Comparison**: Clear pricing and service level comparisons
- **Booking System**: Integrated scheduling with calendar availability
- **Equipment Brands**: Showcases major HVAC brand partnerships
- **Energy Savings Calculator**: Interactive tool for cost savings

### Technical Requirements

- Requires booking system integration (Calendly, Acuity, or custom)
- Email automation for seasonal reminders
- Customer portal access for service history
- Mobile-responsive design for field technician access
